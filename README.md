# Cinerune

Cinerune is a simple movie and TV show streaming-style website. You can search for titles, browse by genre or country, open a watch page, save bookmarks, and keep track of what you were watching.

## What You Can Do

- Search for movies and TV shows
- Browse by genre or country
- View top rated movies and shows
- Watch movies in the embedded player
- Pick seasons and episodes for TV shows
- Save titles to bookmarks
- Mark titles as Watching, Watched, Plan to Watch, or Dropped
- Continue from recently watched titles
- Create an account to sync progress
- Report a broken stream or title issue

## How To Use It

Open Cinerune and use the search bar at the top to find a movie or show. You can also use the Genre, Countries, and Top Rated links to discover something without searching.

Click any poster to open its watch page. For movies, the player loads directly. For TV shows, use the season selector and episode buttons to choose what to watch.

## Bookmarks

On a watch page, click Bookmark and choose a list:

- Watching
- Watched
- Plan to Watch
- Dropped

Your saved titles appear on the Bookmarks page. If you remove a bookmark, it disappears from that list.

## Continue Watching

Cinerune saves watch progress so you can come back later. If you are signed in, your progress can sync with your account. If you are not signed in, progress is saved only on the device and browser you are using.

## Accounts

You can create an account with a username and password. Email is optional. Signing in lets Cinerune keep bookmarks and watch progress connected to your account.

From the account menu, you can update your username, add an email, change your password, pick an avatar, or sign out.

## Reports

If a stream is broken or the wrong title loads, open the watch page and click Report. Write what is wrong so it can be checked.

## Notes

Some videos are provided through embedded players. If a video does not load, try refreshing the page, turning off aggressive ad blocking for the site, or using the Report button.
