# Cinerune

##Link: https://cinerune.extraburner8.workers.dev

Cinerune is a movie and TV show streaming-style website. You can search for titles, browse by genre or country, open a watch page, save bookmarks, and keep track of what you were watching.

## What You Can Do

- Search for movies and TV shows
- Browse by genre or country
- View top rated movies and shows separately for movies and TV
- Watch titles in the embedded player with multiple server options
- Pick seasons and episodes for TV shows
- Save titles to bookmarks
- Mark titles as Watching, Watched, Plan to Watch, or Dropped
- Continue from recently watched titles directly from the homepage
- Get notified when new episodes release for shows you are watching
- Create an account to sync progress and bookmarks across devices
- Report a broken stream or title issue

## How To Use It

Open Cinerune and use the search bar at the top to find a movie or show. You can also use the Genre, Countries, and Top Rated links to discover something without searching.

Click any poster to open its watch page. For movies, the player loads directly. For TV shows, use the season selector and episode buttons to choose what to watch.

If a server is not working, use the server buttons below the player to switch between Server 1, Server 2, and Server 3.

## Bookmarks

On a watch page, click Bookmark and choose a list:

- Watching
- Watched
- Plan to Watch
- Dropped

Your saved titles appear on the Bookmarks page. If you remove a bookmark, it disappears from that list.

## Continue Watching

Cinerune saves watch progress automatically. The homepage shows a Continue Watching row with your recent titles. Clicking an entry takes you back to exactly where you left off, including the correct season and episode for TV shows.

If you are signed in, your progress syncs with your account and is available on any device. If you are not signed in, progress is saved only on the device and browser you are using.

## Notifications

If you have a show saved to your Watching list, Cinerune will notify you when new episodes become available. Check the bell icon in the top navigation to see new episode alerts.

## Accounts

You can create an account with a username and password. Email is optional at signup but can be added later from your account settings.

Signing in lets Cinerune keep your bookmarks and watch progress connected to your account across devices.

From the account menu you can:

- Update your username
- Add or change your email
- Change your password
- Pick an avatar
- Sign out

## Reports

If a stream is broken or the wrong title loads, open the watch page and click Report. Write what is wrong so it can be checked.

## Notes

Some videos are provided through embedded players. If a video does not load, try switching servers using the server buttons below the player before reporting. You can also try refreshing the page or turning off aggressive ad blocking for the site.
